About

Proof that the right
framework beats budget.

Jay Thakkar built the Jay Thakkar Show from the ground up — no studio, no ad spend, no production agency — into a podcast ranked in the top 40 in India (2022) and top 100 across three countries (2023). Episodes drew world-class academic and industry guests. The results were real. The overhead was not.

The lesson: most organisations are already sitting on the content, the credibility, and the audience. What's missing is the framework to extract it, shape it, and put it in front of the right people — consistently, without inflating the budget.

That is what this consultancy was built to deliver.

Why It Matters

Most experts are invisible — not because they lack insight, but because no one taught them how to communicate it.

Whether you are a founder with a decade of hard-won expertise, an academic with research that deserves a wider audience, or a consultant whose clients don't fully understand the value you deliver — the problem is almost never substance. It is always story. The frameworks in this consultancy exist to close that gap, at a fraction of what a traditional agency would charge.
