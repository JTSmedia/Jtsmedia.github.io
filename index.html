The Core Belief

Storytelling Is the Highest-Leverage Skill You're Not Teaching Your Team

Whether you're a postgrad trying to communicate your research, an SME owner posting on LinkedIn, or a university department building its public profile — the bottleneck is never effort. It's always story. The ability to frame an idea so clearly that the right person acts on it immediately.

Pair that with AI tools — most of them free — integrated into your existing workflow, and you get a communication operation that produces more, reaches further, and costs significantly less than what you're spending right now. No new hires. No agencies. Just a smarter system, built around your voice.

This is not theory. Jay ranked a podcast in the global top 100 doing exactly this. The workshop teaches you how to replicate that thinking in your own context.
