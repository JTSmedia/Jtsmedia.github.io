Intellectual Methodology

The Samkhya Framework Applied to Modern Communication

Drawing on the ancient Indian philosophical system of Samkhya — which distinguishes between fundamental principles of consciousness and matter — Jay applies this analytical rigour to diagnose and resolve communication bottlenecks that conventional frameworks miss. The result: positioning, messaging, and narrative architectures built with genuine intellectual foundation, not surface-level strategy.
